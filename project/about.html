About Ghanaian Street Food Guide

This project celebrates the rich and vibrant street food culture of Ghana. Our mission is to promote local cuisine, educate visitors, and highlight the cultural roots behind every delicious bite.

Project Background

This guide was created in 2025 by a passionate software development student to spotlight Ghana’s culinary brilliance. Whether you’re local or a traveler, we aim to help you explore street food with confidence and joy.

Meet the Creator

Patrick Ankomah

A creative coder and street food lover. Kojo built this guide to blend cultural appreciation with web tech.
