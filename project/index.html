Why Street Food?

Street food in Ghana isn’t just a meal — it’s an experience. From the savory spice of Jollof to the comforting taste of Kenkey and Red Red, each bite carries the warmth of Ghanaian culture and tradition.

Must-Try Dishes

Jollof

Flavorful rice cooked in tomato stew with spices and served with meat, fish, or salad.

Red Red

Stewed black-eyed beans with red palm oil, often served with fried plantain and gari.

Kenkey

Fermented corn dough dumpling served with pepper sauce, fried fish, or stew.
